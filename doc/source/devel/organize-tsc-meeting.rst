Organize TSC meeting
====================

Technical Steering Committee (TSC) should meet at least once per quarter.

* *Schedule Meeting* on LFX Project Control Center

  * Title: Modules TSC
  * Show in public calendar: yes
  * Restrict to invited users: no
  * Type: Technical
  * Description::

      Modules Technical Steering Committee meeting

      Agenda:
      * Project news
      * Recent developments
      * ...
      * Q&A

  * Committees: Modules-TSC

* Update `Wiki Home page`_:

  * Change Date, Agenda and Link in *Next TSC meeting* section
  * Add link to new *TSC meeting notes* page in *TSC meeting notes* section

.. _Wiki Home page: https://github.com/envmodules/modules/wiki

* Create ``TSC_meeting_notes_YYYYMMDD`` Wiki page

  * Reuse Table Of Content of last TSC meeting notes page to keep same kind
    of structure and content for each meetings
  * Prepare content to present at the meeting directly in this page

* Advertise meeting

  * At least on `modules-interest mailing list`_ and `Modules chat room`_
  * Indicate meeting date and link to `Wiki Home page`_ to get virtual meeting
    link

.. _modules-interest mailing list: https://lists.hpsf.io/g/modules-interest
.. _Modules chat room: https://matrix.to/#/#modules:matrix.org
